Humidity


Generally it seems that the humidity is highest at Latitude=0. Humidity will overall decrease as we move away from the zero point, however there are outliers to this, it's not a very strong correlation. Such thing as cold, humid environments can exist, large bodies of water can effect city humidity too. So it's not necessarily always the case that as you move away from the equator humidity will increase.
